# Tableau Executive Dashboard

## Project Overview
The **Tableau Executive Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence dashboard designed to provide senior management with a clear and consolidated view of organizational performance.  
It focuses on **revenue, customers, orders, profitability, and sales performance** across multiple dimensions.

This dashboard helps decision-makers quickly identify trends, top performers, and growth opportunities.


## Objectives
- Provide a high-level executive summary of business performance
- Analyze revenue trends by year and month
- Compare performance across states, stores, brands, and categories
- Identify top customers and top-performing sales representatives
- Enable data-driven decision-making through interactive visuals


## Key Metrics Displayed
- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Units Sold**
- **Total Orders**
- **Total Customers**
